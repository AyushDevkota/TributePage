Roman Abramovich

Roman Abramovich, billionaire businessman, politician and owner of Chelsea Football Club

Here's a time line of Roman Abramovich's life:

  • 1966 - Born in Saratov, Russia
  • 1988 - Sets up a company making dolls with Olga
  • 1992 - arrested and sent to prison in a case of theft of government property
  • 1993 - meets Berezovsky and acquire oil company Sibneft
  • 2000 - Sibneft anually produced US$3 billion worth of oil
  • 2003 - became the owner of the companies that control Chelsea Football Club in West London
  • 2015 - invested and led a $30 million round of funding in technology
  • 2019 - net worth of US$12.9 billion making him 113th-richest person in the world.
